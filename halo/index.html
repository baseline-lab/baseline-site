Halo · iPhone

See your gum health improve, week by week.

Take a quick photo of your gums and Halo highlights the visible signs of gum health — redness, swelling, and bleeding — then tracks the trend over time alongside a simple daily routine. Small habit, visible progress.

COMING SOON ON THEApp Store

What Halo does

  • Photo-based gum check with an easy-to-read score and plain-language observations
  • Daily routine tracking and a simple self-reported bleeding check-in
  • Weekly re-scans so you can watch your progress trend
  • A guided water-flossing habit, built for consistency over intensity

Halo is a wellness tool, not a medical device. It helps you observe and track the visible signs of gum health. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any disease and is not a substitute for professional dental care. Seek prompt care for pain, significant bleeding, or other concerning symptoms.
