Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Baseline Labs LLC (“Halo,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Halo mobile app. We collect only what we need to run the app, and we do not sell your personal data.

Halo is a wellness tool, not a medical device. It helps you observe and track the visible signs of gum health (such as redness, swelling, and bleeding). It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any disease, and it is not a substitute for professional dental or medical care.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information, all tied to your account:

Account and profile

  • Your name and email address, provided when you sign in with Apple or Google.
  • An account identifier we use to associate your data with you.

Health-related information you provide

  • Gum photos you capture or upload for analysis.
  • Scan results generated from those photos, including a gum-health score and observations (for example, estimated redness, swelling, and plaque).
  • Care-routine activity you log, such as brushing and flossing check-ins and self-reported gum bleeding.
  • Onboarding answers you choose, such as your brushing/flossing frequency and goals.

We treat your photos and gum-health information as sensitive and use them only to provide the app’s features to you.

Subscription and purchase information

  • Your subscription status and plan. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store; we receive confirmation of your subscription status but never receive your full payment card or Apple ID credentials.

Usage and device information

  • Basic analytics about how you use the app (for example, screens viewed and features used) and limited device/technical information, used to fix bugs and improve the product.

Halo does not collect your precise location, contacts, health-app records, or browsing history, and we do not use your data for third-party advertising or tracking across other companies’ apps.

2. How we use your information

  • To analyze your gum photos and generate your scores, observations, and trends over time.
  • To save your scan history and routine so you can track progress.
  • To provide your subscription and unlock paid features.
  • To send the reminders you enable.
  • To operate, secure, debug, and improve the app.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to your requests.

3. How your photos are analyzed and stored

When you run a scan, your gum photo is transmitted securely to our automated image-analysis provider to generate a score and observations. Your photos and results are stored in your private account, accessible only to you through the app. Other users cannot see your records.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share it for advertising. We share information only with service providers (“subprocessors”) that help us operate Halo, and only as needed to provide their service to us:

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights or users’ safety, or in connection with a business transfer (such as a merger or acquisition), in which case we will notify you if required.

5. Data retention and deletion

We keep your information while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and its associated data at any time from Profile → Delete Account in the app, or by emailing us. Deletion removes your scans, photos, routine activity, and account data from our systems, except where we are required to retain certain records by law.

6. Security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and access controls ensure each user can access only their own records. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information using industry-standard safeguards.

7. International data transfers

We and our service providers may process and store your information in the United States and other countries that may have different data-protection laws than where you live. Where required, we take steps to ensure your information receives an adequate level of protection.

8. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Residents of the EEA/UK (GDPR) and California (CCPA/CPRA) have these rights, and we do not “sell” or “share” personal information as those terms are defined. You can exercise most rights directly in the app (including account deletion) or by contacting us at the email below. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

9. Children’s privacy

Halo is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and, where appropriate, by notice in the app. Your continued use of Halo after an update means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact us

Questions or privacy requests? Email us at support@baseline-lab.com.
