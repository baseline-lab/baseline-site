Our app · iPhone

Halo — see your gum health improve, week by week

Halo is a gum-health wellness app. You take a quick photo of your gums, an automated image-analysis model highlights the visible signs of gum health — redness, swelling, and bleeding — and Halo tracks the trend over time alongside a simple daily care routine.

  • Photo-based gum check with an easy-to-read score and observations
  • Daily routine tracking and a self-reported bleeding check-in
  • Weekly re-scans so you can watch your progress
  • Guided water-flossing habit, built for consistency

Halo is a wellness tool, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any disease and is not a substitute for professional dental care.
